About Dekky Voyages Inc.

Dekky Voyages Inc. is an Independent Travel Advisor proudly affiliated with BWJ Travel, giving clients access to trusted suppliers, industry discounts, and professional planning support.

I have been working in the travel industry for over 10 years in Africa, serving international corporate staff from Firestone, Coca-Cola, the United Nations, and government officials. My experience includes booking complex itineraries, group travel, and diplomatic missions.

I hold a professional diploma in Travel and Tourism from the International Air Travel Association (IATA) and the United Federation of Travel Agents' Associations (UFTAA), earned in 2004.

Why Travel with Dekky Voyages Inc.?

As an Independent Travel Advisor, I specialize in custom vacation planning, cruises, group travel, and honeymoon getaways. I work with trusted suppliers to make your dream trip stress-free and affordable. Clients benefit from personalized service, exclusive deals, and expert guidance every step of the way.
